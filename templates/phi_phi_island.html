Phi Phi Islands are a group of islands located in the Andaman Sea, off the coast of Thailand. Known for their crystal-clear waters, stunning beaches, and lush greenery, the islands are a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. Here are some of the things to see and do in Phi Phi Islands:

Things to See and Do in Phi Phi Islands

  • Visit Maya Bay

    Maya Bay is a stunning beach located on the island of Phi Phi Leh. It gained worldwide fame after being featured in the movie "The Beach" starring Leonardo DiCaprio. Visitors can take a boat tour and spend a day exploring the bay and its surroundings.

  • Take a Snorkeling Trip

    The waters around Phi Phi Islands are home to a diverse range of marine life, including colorful fish, sea turtles, and even reef sharks. Visitors can take a snorkeling trip to explore the underwater world and see these creatures up close.

  • Hike to the Viewpoint

    The Phi Phi Islands offer breathtaking views from the top of their hills. Visitors can take a hike to the viewpoint and enjoy panoramic views of the islands and the Andaman Sea.
