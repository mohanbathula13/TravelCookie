Geneva is a beautiful city located in Switzerland, known for its picturesque views of Lake Geneva, rich cultural history, and chocolate-making traditions. Some of the top things to see and do in Geneva include:

Places to Visit in Geneva

  • Jet d'Eau

    Jet d'Eau is a famous landmark in Geneva, located in Lake Geneva. It is a large fountain that shoots water up to 140 meters in the air, and is a must-see attraction when visiting the city.

  • Old Town

    The Old Town of Geneva is a charming area of the city that is home to many historic buildings, museums, and art galleries. It is a great place to take a stroll and explore the city's rich cultural heritage.

  • Chocolaterie Stettler

    Chocolaterie Stettler is a popular chocolate shop in Geneva that has been making delicious chocolates for over 60 years. Visitors can sample a variety of chocolate treats and learn about the chocolate-making process.
