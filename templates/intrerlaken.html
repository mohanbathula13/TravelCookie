Interlaken is a beautiful town located in the heart of Switzerland, known for its stunning mountain views and outdoor activities. Some of the top things to see and do in Interlaken include:

Places to Visit in Interlaken

  • Jungfraujoch

    Jungfraujoch is the highest railway station in Europe, located at an altitude of 3454 meters. Visitors can take a train up to the station and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding mountains and glaciers.

  • Lake Brienz

    Lake Brienz is a beautiful lake located near Interlaken. Visitors can take a boat tour of the lake and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forests.

  • Harder Kulm

    Harder Kulm is a mountain located near Interlaken that offers stunning panoramic views of the town and the surrounding mountains. Visitors can take a funicular up to the top of the mountain and enjoy the view from the observation deck.
