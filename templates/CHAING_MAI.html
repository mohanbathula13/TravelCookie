Chiang Mai is a city located in northern Thailand, known for its rich culture, historic temples, and natural beauty. Here are some of the top things to see and do in Chiang Mai:

Places to Visit in Chiang Mai

  • Doi Suthep

    Doi Suthep is a mountain located just outside of Chiang Mai, and is home to Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, a beautiful Buddhist temple. Visitors can climb the 300 steps to reach the temple, which offers stunning views of the city and surrounding mountains.

  • The Chiang Mai

    The Chiang Mai Night Bazaar is a bustling market that comes alive in the evening, selling everything from traditional Thai handicrafts to designer clothes. It's a great place to shop, eat and soak up the local culture.

  • Elephant Nature Park

    The Elephant Nature Park is a sanctuary for rescued elephants, located about an hour outside of Chiang Mai. Visitors can spend the day interacting with the elephants, learning about their history and behavior, and even help feed them.
