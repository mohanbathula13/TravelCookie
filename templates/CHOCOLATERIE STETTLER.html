Chocolaterie Stettler is a famous chocolate shop located in Interlaken, Switzerland. The shop is known for its high-quality Swiss chocolate and wide selection of chocolate products. Visitors to Chocolaterie Stettler can enjoy many different chocolate treats and learn about the history of Swiss chocolate-making. Some of the things to see and do at Chocolaterie Stettler include:

Things to See and Do at Chocolaterie Stettler

  • Try Different Flavors of Swiss Chocolate

    Chocolaterie Stettler offers a wide range of chocolate flavors, including milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate. Visitors can sample different flavors and choose their favorite to take home as a souvenir.

  • Watch the Chocolate-Making Process

    Visitors to Chocolaterie Stettler can watch the chocolate-making process and learn about the steps involved in creating high-quality Swiss chocolate. The shop also offers workshops where visitors can make their own chocolate treats.

  • Shop for Chocolate Souvenirs

    Chocolaterie Stettler offers a wide range of chocolate products, including chocolate bars, truffles, and gift boxes. Visitors can purchase chocolate souvenirs to take home for themselves or as gifts for friends and family.
