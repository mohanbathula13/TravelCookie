Big Buddha is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Phuket, Thailand. Located on the top of Nakkerd Hills, it is a giant statue of Buddha made of white marble. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the island from the top of the hill.

Things to See and Do at Big Buddha

  • Visit the Big Buddha Statue

    The Big Buddha statue is the main attraction at the site. Visitors can admire its impressive size and the intricate carvings on the statue.

  • Enjoy the Scenic Views

    The top of Nakkerd Hills offers breathtaking views of Phuket and the surrounding areas. Visitors can take a moment to relax and take in the beautiful scenery.

  • Learn About Buddhism

    The site also has several small temples and shrines where visitors can learn about Buddhism and its practices.
