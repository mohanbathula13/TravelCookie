Lake Brienz is a stunning lake located in Interlaken, Switzerland. The lake is known for its crystal-clear blue waters and breathtaking mountain views. Visitors to Lake Brienz can enjoy many different activities and attractions, including:

Things to See and Do at Lake Brienz

  • Boat Tours

    Visitors can take a boat tour on Lake Brienz to enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and countryside. There are several tour options available, including short tours and full-day tours.

  • Hiking and Biking

    The area around Lake Brienz is perfect for hiking and biking, with many scenic trails to choose from. Visitors can rent bikes or join a guided hike to explore the beautiful Swiss countryside.

  • Swimming and Water Sports

    Lake Brienz is a great place for swimming and other water sports, with its clear waters and beautiful surroundings. Visitors can rent paddle boats or kayaks to explore the lake, or simply relax on the beach and soak up the sun.
